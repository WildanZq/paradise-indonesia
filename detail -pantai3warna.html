3 COLOR BEACH

Malang, East Java

4,6 10.029 review

The beauty of the beach in Indonesia is not lost with the beaches in foreign countries. Even recently enough attention of foreign tourists. One is a beach located in Malang, East Java. The beach is still rarely exposed and still maintained its authenticity are the three colors of the beach located in the Sitiarjo village , Sumbermanjing subdistrict, Malang. This unique beach have three colors: red, green and blue when viewed from a distance.

You must be wondering, why this beach can have three colors? Here is a scientific explanation:
Sun visors consists of seven colors (red, orange, yellow, green, blue, indigo, ultraviolet). Each color has its own wavelength. The ability of light to penetrate the water depends on the wavelength. The shorter the wave of light, the greater its power to penetrate the water. Therefore, the red light will be absorbed at a depth less than 20 meters, and after that its existence is hidden or invisible. Here began to appear dark red color.

On the green sea, because of the mud that is deposited near the shore which reflect the color green and also for their plankton in large quantities. While on the ocean blue, this is caused by the short-wave sunlight (blue light) reflected more than in any other light.

If you want a vacation, there's no harm in trying traveled to this beach with people you care about. The location is about 150 km from the city of Malang. The access road which is taken from Malang about two hours using a vehicle.

Entrance tickets to be paid by visitors is very affordable, which is about Rp 6,000 includes the cost of parking which managed by local residents. Cheap is not it? If you want tracking with short lines or long lines at the beach, you can hire a guide to deliver from the beginning to the post towards the coast at a cost of Rp 75,000. The definition of a short path is a direct path toward the beach three colors traversed approximately less than two km from the Perhutani post or of a motorcycle parking.
