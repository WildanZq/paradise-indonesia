SATAY

MEAT | HALAL FOOD | ETC

In Indonesia, sate ayam (chicken satay) is the most common form of the dish, served with lontong or compressed rice cakes. The national condiment, kecap manis, is drizzled over the skewers accompanied with a spicy peanut sauce, eschalot and cucumber.
